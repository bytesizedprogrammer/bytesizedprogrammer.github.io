Project Details:

I am currently developing an E-Commerce website for Homeric Imports, designed to enhance their online presence and streamline the shopping experience for customers. This project is in the final stages of development and will soon undergo rigorous testing by cybersecurity professionals to ensure robust security measures are in place.

Due to the sensitive nature of the project, specific details are withheld for security purposes. However, I welcome inquiries from potential employers interested in the website's functionality. You can reach out through the contact information provided at the bottom of the home page, or feel free to explore the website once it launches.



Resume points:
  • Designed and developed the graphical design for the website
  • Made a register/login system for users and admin creation on the admin end
  • Created a customized algorithm to personalize user experiences based on data, improving engagement and satisfaction
  • Designed and developed a comprehensive admin dashboard, integrating critical business metrics including sales data, top customers, and profitability, to support strategic decision-making along with making updating product data, promotions, etc. simple
  • Developed and managed multiple CRUD APIs to handle products, promotions, and user data, utilizing data insights to streamline product management and promotional strategies
  • Implemented data collection strategies to monitor product sales, returns, and user purchasing behavior, leading to enhanced marketing strategies
  • Created dashboards for stakeholders to visualize product performance, including best-selling products and user purchasing patterns
